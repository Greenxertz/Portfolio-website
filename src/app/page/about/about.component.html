So why Greenxertz and a duck logo?

Here’s a funny story: my favorite color is Green, and Xertz is a quirky word my coach coined for me after a regatta. It means to gulp water extremely quickly- he called me Xertzy. When I googled the term and found no results, I decided to embrace it. Now, if you search for Greenxertz, Most of the results are linked to me. Neat, right?
As for the duck logo, there's an inside joke from high school where we had to bring a green rubber duck to someone’s house. It stuck, and now, I have my green rubber duck for debugging.
